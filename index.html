About Your Practitioner

Meet Jilla Malek-Mohammadi

With over 20 years of experience as a UK Licensed Medical Aesthetician, Jilla brings precision-perfect expertise to every treatment. Her philosophy is simple: to create the best possible version of you — without surgery or excisions.

Specialising in electro micro surgery, medical micropigmentation, and advanced cosmetic procedures, Jilla combines clinical precision with an artist's eye. Her international clientele travel from the Cayman Islands, Bermuda, Dubai, and beyond.
