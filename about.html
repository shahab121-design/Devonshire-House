Your Practitioner

Jilla Malek-Mohammadi

UK Licensed Medical Aesthetician with over 20 years of specialist experience.

Jilla is a pioneering practitioner in the field of non-surgical aesthetics, specialising in electro micro surgery for the cosmetic removal of benign growths. Her precision-perfect technique and artistic eye have attracted an international clientele who travel from the Cayman Islands, Bermuda, Dubai, Spain, and across London.

Her philosophy is simple yet transformative: to create the best possible version of you — without surgery, without excisions, and with minimal downtime. Every treatment is approached with the care and precision of an artist, ensuring natural-looking, confidence-boosting results.

Our Philosophy

The Best Possible Version of You

At Devonshire House, we believe that everyone deserves to feel confident in their own skin. Our approach is rooted in precision, artistry, and a deep understanding of skin science.
